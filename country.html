The Country

India is a nation in South Asia, formally known as the Republic of India. It is the world's most populated democracy since gaining independence in 1947 and is currently the seventh-largest country by area as of June 2023. Its land boundaries are shared with Pakistan to the west; China, Nepal, and Bhutan to the north; Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east; and the Indian Ocean, the Arabian Sea, and the Bay of Bengal to the southeast. India's Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime boundary with Thailand, Myanmar, and Indonesia. It is located in the Indian Ocean near Sri Lanka and the Maldives.
